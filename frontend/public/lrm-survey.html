LRM search experience survey

We kindly invite you to participate in a task-based survey with the purpose of exploring various aspects of bibliographic data. We do not expect any particular knowledge in advance. In the survey you will be asked to perform specific tasks and judgements related to specific queries.

The survey system

The survey system mimics a library search system where each entry in the search result represents a grouping based on the content of publications. For each task, you will get a predefined query and result listing (using a predefined link). Each entry can be expanded to what items are available for this content using the "Available as: " option. On the top of the page is a button that can be used to hide and show all available items.

To the left of the display, under the icons indicating content type, there are two circular buttons with arrows that can be used to indicate how you rank items according to the task given. The up arrow is used to give a positive ranking, the down arrow to give a negative ranking. Ranked items are marked with green for positive and red for negative. A ranking can be cancelled by clicking on the cancel button.

When you have completed the task, click on the "Submit" button to send your feedback. This will show a dialog where you have to rank your own expertise.
